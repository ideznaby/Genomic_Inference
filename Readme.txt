The UtahfamilySNPdata,which is the result of the preprocessing is the 1000 SNPs of 22nd Genome of the CEPH/Utah pedigree 1463.
The first column is the SNP position and the rest 17 column is the frequency of minor alleles for each family member.
However, we only used 11 member(11 column of the Allele.txt).
